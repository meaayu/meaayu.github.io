About Me

"Making things feel alive — that's what I'm here for."

I'm Aayu — a developer and artist who believes the best digital experiences are ones that feel handmade. I work at the intersection of code, drawing, and motion.

My workflow starts with pencil on paper — rough sketches, gestural thumbnails, quick storyboards. Then I translate that energy into pixels, keeping all the imperfection and warmth.

When I'm not pushing commits or animating frames, you'll find me filling sketchbooks, experimenting with watercolor, or studying how light behaves at golden hour.
